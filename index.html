Our Services

Comprehensive water solutions tailored to meet all your needs with professional expertise and modern equipment.

New Borewell Drilling

Professional drilling services with modern equipment for reliable water access. Our expert team ensures quality drilling with comprehensive site assessment.

  • Deep bore drilling up to 1000 feet
  • Site survey and geological assessment
  • Quality assurance and testing

Old Borewell Solutions

Expert solutions to restore and revitalize your old borewells for optimal performance. We bring new life to existing water sources.

  • Borewell cleaning and maintenance
  • Yield improvement techniques
  • Pump replacement and upgrades

Pump Installation

Expert pump installation and maintenance services for optimal water flow. We ensure efficient water delivery systems for all your needs.

  • Submersible pump installation
  • Centrifugal pump systems
  • Energy-efficient solutions

Maintenance & Repair

Comprehensive maintenance and repair services to keep your borewell system running efficiently. We provide preventive care and emergency repairs.

  • Complete system diagnostics
  • Emergency repair services
  • Preventive maintenance programs

Water Quality Testing

Professional water quality testing and analysis services to ensure safe, clean drinking water. We provide comprehensive testing and certification.

  • Complete chemical analysis
  • pH and mineral testing
  • Contamination detection
